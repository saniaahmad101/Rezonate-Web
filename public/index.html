About Rezonate

Rezonate is built to help you track, reflect, and build healthier routines with a calm design and a privacy-first mindset.

What it helps with

Use quick daily check-ins to track mental health, sleep, habits, and journaling. Review patterns over time to understand what supports you and what gets in the way.

  • Simple daily logging
  • Trends you can review over time
  • Supportive tools in one place

  • Calm, modern UI
  • Private-first controls
  • Progress that feels encouraging
